Frequently Asked Questions

Is ItinFinder free?

Yes, every user gets 5 free AI Itinery generation requests per day. You can watch rewarded ads for extra credits or upgrade to a Pro plan for unlimited AI generations and you unlock deep mode.

How accurate is Deep Mode?

Deep Mode uses TinyFish agents to verify live data (opening hours, availability) with over 97% accuracy. Results may vary during network issues.

Can I use ItinFinder offline?

Absolutely. ItinFinder supports offline mode to store requests locally. It will autosync with database when you're back online.

What languages are supported?

English, Spanish, French, German, and Japanese. More coming soon.
